The Project:

I was tired of how bare my ceiling looked one day and decided it needed something moving and eye catching. Thus was born the String Walker. It is another simple gizmo design that holds a motor in a bracket driving a conical wheel with central ribbing to grab onto string. The top bracket also holds a carriage which is what carries along an Arduino Uno, DC Motor driver board, and power supply. The triangular bracket system allowed for easy balancing of the unit as the carriageâ€™s weight could be easily shifted to one side or the other. This bracket system is also a great system to use on other projects.

At a Glance:

  • 3D Modeled and printed motor bracket and triangular adjusting mounting bracket
  • Used Arduino IDE and Arduino Uno to drive DC Motor
