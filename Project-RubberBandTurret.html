The Project:

I never thought I could underestimate the power of a Nerf trigger. Originally designed to hold a Nerf gun, this project shifted to a rubber band turret after the trigger proved to require more force than small servos could handle. This project used laser cut materials which proved to be a great way to quickly get a solid structure. This was made with servos controlling a rotating base, the pitch of the rubber band gun, and the releasing of the rubber band. All was controlled with a joystick.

At a Glance:

  • Designed stand in Solidworks and laser cut the result
  • Soldered Arduino shield to replace breadboard and drive 3 servos and take input from a joystick
