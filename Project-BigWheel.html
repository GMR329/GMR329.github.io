The Project:

This One Wheel is a very simple build where all components are contained within the wheel itself allowing for it to self balance. The distribution of internal components is what ultimately drives it forward. It uses a small DC motor to rotate a wheel shell and then “falls” forward. This project had a number of early failures due to poor sizing with a round housing structure but ultimately turned in to an entertaining driving gizmo.

At a Glance:

  • 3D modeled and printed wheel shell and internal housing unit
  • Programmed controls with Arduino IDE and ran off of an Arduino Nano
  • Iterated over housing failures with different component selection and print testing
